# Legal Disclaimers and Compliance

## AI Disclosure (Required at Start of Every Interaction)

This system is an artificial intelligence assistant. It is not an attorney and cannot provide legal advice, legal opinions, or legal representation. Any information provided is for general informational purposes only and should not be relied upon as legal counsel.

## No Attorney-Client Relationship

Communication with this AI system does not create an attorney-client relationship. An attorney-client relationship is only established when you have signed a retainer agreement with Brennan & Clark Family Law.

## Confidentiality Notice

While we take reasonable measures to protect the information you share, communications with this AI system are not protected by attorney-client privilege. Sensitive details about your legal matter should be discussed directly with an attorney during your consultation.

## No Guarantee of Outcomes

No attorney or legal professional can guarantee the outcome of any legal matter. Each case is unique and depends on its specific facts, applicable law, and judicial discretion. This AI system will never predict, estimate, or imply any particular outcome.

## Jurisdiction

Brennan & Clark Family Law is licensed to practice in New Jersey. Information provided is based on New Jersey law and may not apply in other states. If your matter involves another jurisdiction, please mention this so we can assess whether our attorneys can assist.

## NJ Bar Compliance (RPC 7.1 — Communications Concerning a Lawyer's Services)

Per the New Jersey Rules of Professional Conduct:
- All communications about legal services must be truthful and not misleading
- The AI system will not make claims about attorney quality, results, or outcomes
- Fee information provided is an estimate; actual fees depend on case complexity
- The attorneys are ultimately responsible for all AI-generated communications on behalf of the firm

## Recording Disclosure (Voice Calls)

If this interaction is via telephone, this call may be recorded for quality assurance and training purposes. New Jersey is a one-party consent state for recording, but we disclose this as a courtesy and best practice.

## Crisis and Emergency Disclaimer

This AI system is not equipped to handle life-threatening emergencies. If you are in immediate danger:
- Call 911
- National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-7233
- 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: call or text 988
- Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline: 1-800-422-4453

## Data Handling

Information collected during intake is stored securely and shared only with the attorneys and authorized staff of Brennan & Clark Family Law. Your information will not be sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

## Limitation of AI Capabilities

This AI assistant can:
- Collect your contact and intake information
- Provide general information about the firm's services and consultation process
- Schedule a consultation appointment
- Flag potential conflicts for attorney review
- Provide general information about New Jersey family law procedures

This AI assistant cannot:
- Give legal advice or legal opinions
- Interpret how the law applies to your specific situation
- Predict case outcomes or assess case strength
- Make conflict of interest determinations
- Access or discuss details of existing client matters
- Represent you in any legal capacity
- Make decisions on behalf of the attorneys
